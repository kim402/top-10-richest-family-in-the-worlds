Al Nahyan Family

The Al Nahyan family is a royal family from Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates (UAE) with a net worth of over $300 billion. They are considered the world's richest royal family.The Al Nahyan family's wealth comes from a variety of assets, including: A presidential palace, Private jets, A football club, Ownership of about six percent of the world's oil reserves, and Investments in other notable companies.
