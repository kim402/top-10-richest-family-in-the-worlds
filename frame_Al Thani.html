Al Thani Family

The Al Thani family is the ruling family of Qatar and is part of the Tamimi tribe. The family's origins can be traced back to the Adnanite Banu Tamim tribe.The current emir of Qatar, Tamim is the youngest son of Sheikh Hamad bin Khalifa Al Thani and his second wife, Sheikha Mozah bint Nasser al-Missned. He became heir apparent in 2003 after his older brother renounced his claim to the throne
