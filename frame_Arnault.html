Arnault Family

The Arnault family is a wealthy family of French businesspeople.Bernard Arnault The head of the Arnault family, Bernard was born in 1949 in Roubaix, France to Jean Léon Arnault, a manufacturer, and Marie-Josèphe Savinel, a pianist. Bernard was the first in his family to attend university, graduating with honors from the prestigious Ecole Polytechnique in Paris. He joined his father's business as the chief construction officer and later became the CEO of Dior. Bernard has five children, four of whom work in the family business.Jean Arnault The youngest son of Bernard and his second wife, Hélène Mercier, Jean works in the watch division of Louis Vuitton.Alexandre Arnault The president of Tiffany & Co. in New York City, Alexandre is the older brother of Frédéric and Jean. Frédéric Arnault The second son of Bernard and Hélène Mercier. Delphine Arnault One of Bernard's two children with his first wife, Anne Dewavrin.Antoine Arnault The Vice-Chairman and CEO of Christian Dior SE, Antoine is one of Bernard's two children with Anne Dewavrin.
