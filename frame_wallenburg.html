Wallenburg Family

The Wallenberg family is a prominent Swedish family renowned as bankers, industrialists, politicians, bureaucrats and diplomats,The Wallenberg family's business empire includes holdings in AstraZeneca, Ericsson, Saab and Nasdaq, and is worth 250 billion euros ($278 billion), according to the Financial Times,The Wallenbergs are not only Sweden's most influential family, but also rank among the most powerful in the world. The empire is currently steered by the fifth generation: brothers Jacob Wallenberg and Peter Wallenberg Jr., along with their cousin Marcus Wallenberg
